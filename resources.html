Materials for passionate learners

Here are related educational resources and lists of certain platforms that you could visit to begin your New Skill journey.




  1. edX

    2. Partnering with the world's leading universities, edX offers online courses for various subjects. Classes are sourced directly from top institutions such as Amazon, Harvard, MIT, and Microsoft. They are usually made up of weekly modules which enrolled students can go through at their own pace. Assessments and pre-recorded videos are also included. While edX's courses are free, you have to pay a fixed fee for graded homework and unlimited course access, and to receive an official certificate upon completing your chosen course.



  2. Coursera

    3. Coursera provides access to a wide range of courses from personal development to soft-skills needed in our daily lives. Moreover, the platform offers bachelor's and master's degree programs with the aim of reducing barriers to higher education. Similar to edX, Coursera is mostly free, however, students are required to pay for the course if they would prefer getting a certificate or would like to sign up for classes that cover more in-depth materials.



  3. Khan Academy

    4. Need help with the SAT? AP? Khan Academy is an absolute gem for not only high schoolers, but also those in preschool and kindergarten. The site has thousands of articles, practice papers, simulation tests, and videos to guide you on learning new things or simply help you gain additional knowledge on the subjects you have leanred. Everything you need to know about college, careers, and other life skills are accessible as well. Waste no time and choose a topic to study!



  4. Debating404

    5. From a fellow debating enthusiast, Debating404 is the perfect place to find written transcripts from accomplished debaters worldwide. You could make good use of them to further study what constitutes a good debate speech. There are also several motion analyses and case exercises available to hone your thinking skills.



  5. EVERFI

    6. EVERFI provides an interactive learning experience for students. With a research-backed online education curriculum, students and educators can gain access to materials on financial literacy, wellness, compliance, and other essential life skills. There is often a glossary included to look up terms that students do not understand. Additionally, they can go back and review sections as needed.



  6. MasterClass

    7. Interested in business? Music? Literature? Or all of them? MasterClass is a paid platform that has pre-recorded video lessons from the world's best. You can watch them anytime and anywhere. They also have Sessions, that is, a program where you can actually learn alongside these influential people by working on projects. Your work will be shared with peers who will be giving you feedback. Classes are designed for all skill levels.
